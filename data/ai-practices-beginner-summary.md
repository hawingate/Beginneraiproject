## What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is when computers learn to do tasks that normally need human intelligence - like recognizing faces, understanding speech, or making decisions. Think of it as teaching computers to be smart helpers!

## Quick Checklist for Beginners

When you hear about or work with AI, ask yourself:

- [ ] Can I understand how it makes decisions?
- [ ] Does it treat everyone fairly?
- [ ] Is my personal data protected?
- [ ] Are humans still making important choices?
- [ ] Has it been tested thoroughly?
- [ ] Does it improve over time?
- [ ] Is it energy-efficient?
- [ ] Did diverse people help create it?

## Bottom Line

**Good AI should be:**
- **Understandable** - You know what it's doing
- **Fair** - Treats everyone equally
- **Safe** - Protects your privacy
- **Helpful** - Makes life better, not worse
- **Responsible** - Has humans overseeing it

**Remember:** AI is a tool. Like any tool, it's not good or bad by itself - it depends on how we build and use it!
